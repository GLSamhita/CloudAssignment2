ABOUT VISHAKAPATNAM (VIZAG)

Visakhapatnam is a port city and industrial center in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh, on the Bay of Bengal. The city is situated between the Eastern Ghats and the Bay of Bengal.
It's known for its many beaches, including Ramakrishna Beach, home to a preserved submarine at the Kursura Submarine Museum. Nearby are the elaborate Kali Temple and the Visakha Museum, an old Dutch bungalow housing local maritime and historical exhibits.
Visakhapatnam has a has a tropical wet and dry climate.
